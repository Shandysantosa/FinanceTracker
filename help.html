Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new transaction?
Go to the Add Transaction page from the sidebar, fill in the required details, and click Add Transaction.
Can I edit or delete a transaction?
Currently, you can delete a transaction from the History page. Editing is not yet supported but will be added in future updates.
How do I change my profile or settings?
Click your profile icon in the top right, then select Profile or Settings from the dropdown menu.
How can I export my transaction history?
On the History page, use the Export CSV or Export Excel buttons to download your data.
Is my data secure?
Your data is stored locally in your browser for privacy. No financial data is sent to any server unless you use the optional PHP backend.
